The Avianca Incident

Our journey to Costa Rica on Avianca Airlines was going smoothly until the final thirty minutes of our first flight, when the undeniable, SpongeBob-style wafts of pure poop started rolling into the back rows of the plane. We survived the first leg, questioning our life choices as the mystery pooper seemingly followed us on both our flights (We didn't know yet).

After ten glorious, stench-free days in paradise, we thought we were safe.

We were completely wrong.

On the final flight back to Toronto, the biological warfare went into absolute overdrive. As flight attendants desperately emptied entire cans of Febreze to combat the putrid cloud.

Finally, one of our flight comrades (lest we forget?) in the back row finally snapped, screaming for all to hear: 'Who the f*** is shitting their pants?! Who sh*t in their pants right now?! There's a f***ing washroom if you need to sh*t yourself, just go in there!' We never found the culprit, but we will never forget the Great Avianca Stench.
